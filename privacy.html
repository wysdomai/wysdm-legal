Privacy Policy for Wysdom AI

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Overview

Wysdom AI (“we,” “our,” “the App”) provides AI-powered study tools that process user-submitted materials. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, processed, transmitted, and safeguarded when you use the App.

Information Collected

  • Authentication information (Apple or Google sign-in, email)
  • User-submitted documents, images, and study materials
  • Derived textual data extracted from submitted materials
  • Usage metrics, analytics data, and feature interaction data
  • Subscription and purchase status

Processing of Submitted Materials

User-submitted documents and images are transmitted to external automated processing systems for the purpose of text extraction, analysis, transformation, and generation of study content. These systems may utilize machine learning technologies to improve the quality, accuracy, and performance of document interpretation and content generation.

By using the App, you acknowledge and consent to this processing, which may occur on infrastructure not operated directly by Wysdom AI and subject to the policies of those service providers.

Use of Information

  • Provide study generation features
  • Transform extracted text into educational content
  • Maintain, improve, and optimize system performance
  • Manage subscriptions and feature access

Data Transmission and Third-Party Processing

The App relies on third-party computational service providers to perform document parsing, text extraction, and AI-driven content generation. Submitted materials and derived text may be processed by these providers as necessary to deliver the App’s functionality.

Wysdom AI does not sell user data. However, data processed by third-party systems is subject to their respective data handling practices.

Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are implemented to protect user data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

User Rights

  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Access your stored information
  • Opt out of analytics collection

Age Requirement

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older.
